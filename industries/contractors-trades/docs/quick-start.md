# Contractors & Trades Template - Quick Start Guide

## Welcome to Your New Website!

This guide will help you customize your Contractors & Trades website template in just a few simple steps. No technical skills required!

---

## Step 1: Replace Company Information

### What to Change:
- **Company Name**: Update everywhere you see "ProCraft Contractors"
- **Phone Number**: Replace with your business phone
- **Email Address**: Add your contact email
- **Business Address**: Update with your location
- **License Number**: Add your contractor license

### Where to Find It:
- Header navigation
- Footer contact information
- Hero section (if mentioned)
- About section (if applicable)

---

## Step 2: Customize the Hero Section

### Hero Image
**What to do:**
1. Find a high-quality photo of your work or team (1920x1080px recommended)
2. Replace the placeholder image
3. Make sure the image shows your best work!

**Pro tip:** Use a photo that shows the transformation you create for customers.

### Hero Text
**What to change:**
- **Main headline**: Keep it under 10 words, focus on benefits
- **Subtitle**: Explain what makes you different (2-3 sentences)
- **Call-to-Action buttons**: Update button text to match your services

**Example:**
- Good: "Expert Kitchen Remodeling You Can Trust"
- Better: "Transform Your Kitchen in 2 Weeks - Licensed & Guaranteed"

---

## Step 3: Update Services Section

### For Each Service:
1. **Service Title**: Clear, benefit-focused name
2. **Description**: 2-3 sentences explaining what you do
3. **Service Image**: Professional photo of completed work
4. **Features List**: 3-4 key benefits or included items

### Service Ideas for Contractors:
- Kitchen & Bathroom Remodeling
- Home Additions & Extensions
- Roofing & Siding
- Deck Construction
- Basement Finishing
- Electrical & Plumbing Updates

**Pro tip:** Focus on the results customers get, not just what you do.

---

## Step 4: Add Your Project Gallery

### Project Photos
**What you need:**
- Before and after photos (if possible)
- High-quality images (1200x800px minimum)
- Show different types of work
- Include variety in project sizes

### For Each Project:
1. **Project Title**: Descriptive name (e.g., "Modern Farmhouse Kitchen")
2. **Description**: Brief overview of what was done
3. **Category**: Kitchen, Bathroom, Addition, Roofing, etc.
4. **Completion Date**: When the project finished

**Pro tip:** Start with your 6 best projects. Add more as you complete new work.

---

## Step 5: Update "Why Choose Us" Section

### Features to Highlight:
- Years of experience
- Licensed and insured status
- Warranty/guarantee information
- Free estimates
- Local expertise
- Customer satisfaction rating

### How to Present:
- Use bullet points or numbered lists
- Include specific numbers (e.g., "15+ Years Experience")
- Add trust badges or certifications
- Mention any awards or affiliations

### Service Options to Include:

---

## Step 7: Add Customer Testimonials

### What Makes a Good Testimonial:
- Specific results mentioned
- Names and locations included
- Recent (within last 2 years)
- Different types of projects represented

### How to Get Testimonials:
- Ask satisfied customers via email
- Include on invoices or contracts
- Use Google Review link in email signature
- Follow up after project completion

---

## Media Guidelines

### Image Sizes & Formats:
- **Hero Image**: 1920x1080px (16:9 ratio)
- **Service Images**: 800x600px (4:3 ratio)
- **Project Images**: 1200x800px (3:2 ratio)
- **Logo**: 300x100px (3:1 ratio)

### File Formats:
- Photos: JPG or WebP (best quality)
- Logos: PNG or SVG (transparent background)
- Max file size: 2MB for hero, 1MB for others

### Image Tips:
- Use natural lighting
- Show clean, professional work
- Include people if it shows scale
- Avoid cluttered backgrounds

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### ❌ Don't:
- Use blurry or low-quality photos
- Write long paragraphs (keep it scannable)
- Forget to update contact information
- Use generic placeholder text
- Include too many services (focus on your strengths)

### ✅ Do:
- Use high-quality, professional images
- Write benefit-focused headlines
- Include specific details and numbers
- Add customer testimonials
- Update all contact information
- Test all links and forms
