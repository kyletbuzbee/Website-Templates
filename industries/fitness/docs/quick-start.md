# Fitness Industry Template - Quick Start Guide

## Welcome to Your Fitness Center Website!

This guide will help you customize your fitness industry template in just a few steps. Perfect for gyms, fitness centers, wellness studios, and health clubs!

---

## Step 1: Gym Information

### What to Change:
- **Gym Name**: Replace "Peak Performance Fitness"
- **Tagline**: Your unique fitness brand message
- **Location**: Your facility address and neighborhood
- **Years Established**: When your gym opened

### Where to Find It:
- Page title and header
- Hero section
- Footer contact information
- About section

---

## Step 2: Hero Section Setup

### Hero Image
**What to do:**
1. Choose an inspiring photo of your gym interior or members working out
2. Use a high-quality image (1920x1080px recommended)
3. Show your equipment and facility

**Pro tip:** Use action shots of members working out to inspire potential clients.

### Hero Text
**What to change:**
- **Main headline**: Focus on transformation and results (under 10 words)
- **Subtitle**: Describe your community and facilities (2-3 sentences)
- **Buttons**: Update to match your membership options

**Example Headlines:**
- "Transform Your Body, Transform Your Life"
- "Where Fitness Meets Community"
- "Achieve Your Peak Performance"

---

## Step 3: Classes Section

### Featured Classes
**For each class:**
1. **Class Name**: Clear, appealing name
2. **Description**: What members will experience
3. **Difficulty Level**: Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced, All Levels
4. **Duration & Instructor**: Class length and who teaches it
5. **Schedule**: When the class is offered

### Class Tips:
- Feature 4-6 popular classes
- Include variety (HIIT, yoga, strength, cardio)
- Update schedules regularly
- Highlight unique or signature classes

---

## Step 4: Trainer Profiles

### Trainer Information
**For each trainer:**
1. **Professional Photo**: Fitness attire, action shot
2. **Name and Specialty**: Their area of expertise
3. **Bio**: Experience, certifications, passion (2-3 sentences)
4. **Certifications**: List relevant credentials

### Getting Photos:
- Professional fitness photography
- Show trainers in action or with clients
- Consistent style across all trainer photos
- High-quality, well-lit images

---

## Step 5: Facilities Showcase

### Facility Photos
**What to include:**
- Main workout floor
- Cardio equipment area
- Strength training zone
- Group fitness studios
- Locker rooms and amenities
- Recovery areas (sauna, lounge)

**Photo Tips:**
- Clean, well-maintained spaces
- Show equipment in use
- Highlight unique features
- Professional lighting

---

## Step 6: Membership Plans

### Pricing Structure
**For each plan:**
1. **Plan Name**: Clear, descriptive name
2. **Price**: Monthly cost (be transparent)
3. **Features**: What's included in each plan
4. **Popular Badge**: Mark your most popular option

### Membership Tips:
- 3-4 pricing tiers work best
- Be clear about what's included
- Consider annual pricing discounts
- Highlight the most popular plan

---

## Step 7: Success Stories

### Member Testimonials
**What to include:**
- Recent success stories (within 6 months)
- Specific results (weight loss, strength gains, etc.)
- Different fitness goals represented
- Member photos (with permission)

### Getting Testimonials:
- Ask satisfied members after goals achieved
- Use email follow-ups
- Create testimonial collection forms
- Offer incentives for sharing stories

---

## Step 8: Join Now Section

### Membership Form
**Form Fields:**
- Name, email, phone (required)
- Membership type selection
- Fitness goals (optional)
- Newsletter signup

### Membership Options:
Match exactly with your pricing plans.

---

## Step 9: Contact Information

### Essential Details:
- Phone number (primary contact)
- Email address
- Physical address with directions
- Hours of operation (including holidays)

### Additional Information:
- Parking information
- Public transportation
- Emergency contact
- Guest policy

---

## Media Guidelines

### Image Sizes & Types:
- **Hero Image**: 1920x1080px (16:9 ratio) - Gym interior or members working out
- **Class Images**: 600x400px (3:2 ratio) - Action shots of classes
- **Trainer Photos**: 400x500px (4:5 ratio) - Professional fitness portraits
- **Facility Photos**: 800x600px (4:3 ratio) - Equipment and amenities
- **Logo**: 200x80px (2.5:1 ratio) - Gym branding

### Photo Tips:
- Show real members working out
- Professional, motivational imagery
- Clean, modern facility shots
- Diverse representation

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### ❌ Don't:
- Use stock photos that don't match your gym
- Overpromise results or weight loss
- Forget to update class schedules
- Hide pricing or membership details
- Ignore mobile users (many search on phones)

### ✅ Do:
- Use authentic photos of your facility
- Be transparent about pricing and policies
- Keep class information current
- Highlight your unique features
- Regularly update member success stories
