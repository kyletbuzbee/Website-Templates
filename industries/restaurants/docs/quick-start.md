# Restaurants Industry Template - Quick Start Guide

## Welcome to Your Restaurant Website!

This guide will help you customize your restaurant industry template in just a few steps. Perfect for restaurants, cafes, bistros, and dining establishments!

---

## Step 1: Restaurant Information

### What to Change:
- **Restaurant Name**: Replace "Bella Vista Restaurant"
- **Cuisine Type**: Italian, American, Mexican, etc.
- **Tagline**: Your unique selling proposition
- **Location**: Your restaurant's address and neighborhood

### Where to Find It:
- Page title and header
- Hero section
- Footer contact information
- About section

---

## Step 2: Hero Section Setup

### Hero Image
**What to do:**
1. Choose a stunning photo of your restaurant interior or signature dish
2. Use a high-quality image (1920x1080px recommended)
3. Show your restaurant's ambiance and style

**Pro tip:** Use an image that captures the mood you want customers to experience.

### Hero Text
**What to change:**
- **Main headline**: Focus on your unique appeal (under 10 words)
- **Subtitle**: Describe your cuisine and atmosphere (2-3 sentences)
- **Buttons**: Update to match your restaurant's services

**Example Headlines:**
- "Authentic Italian Dining Experience"
- "Farm-to-Table American Cuisine"
- "Traditional Mexican Restaurant & Bar"

---

## Step 3: Menu Section

### Featured Dishes
**For each dish:**
1. **Dish Name**: Appetizing and descriptive
2. **Description**: Highlight key ingredients and preparation
3. **Price**: Current menu pricing
4. **Tags**: Dietary info, popularity, specials

### Menu Tips:
- Feature 4-6 signature dishes
- Include variety (appetizers, mains, desserts)
- Update prices regularly
- Add seasonal specials

---

## Step 4: About Section

### Restaurant Story
**What to include:**
- How long you've been in business
- Your culinary philosophy
- What makes your restaurant special
- Awards or recognitions

### Chef/Team Photos
- Professional headshots
- Show your culinary team
- Include brief bios if space allows

---

## Step 5: Atmosphere Gallery

### Restaurant Photos
**What to include:**
- Main dining room
- Bar/lounge area
- Outdoor seating (if applicable)
- Private dining rooms
- Kitchen glimpses (optional)

**Photo Tips:**
- Well-lit, high-quality images
- Show different times of day
- Include people enjoying meals
- Highlight unique features

---

## Step 6: Reservations System

### Hours of Operation
**Update for:**
- Regular business hours
- Holiday schedules
- Special events
- Last seating times

### Reservation Form
**Customize:**
- Party size options (1-8+ guests)
- Time slots based on your schedule
- Special occasion types
- Dietary restriction notes

---

## Step 7: Reviews & Testimonials

### Customer Reviews
**What to include:**
- Recent reviews (within 6 months)
- Mix of platforms (Yelp, TripAdvisor, Google)
- Different types of visits (dates, families, business)
- Both positive and constructive feedback

### Getting Reviews:
- Ask satisfied customers in person
- Follow up via email after visits
- Use review collection services
- Respond to all reviews professionally

---

## Step 8: Contact Information

### Essential Details:
- Phone number (primary contact)
- Email address
- Physical address with directions
- Website and social media links

### Additional Info:
- Parking information
- Public transportation access
- Accessibility features
- Dress code (if applicable)

---

## Media Guidelines

### Image Sizes & Types:
- **Hero Image**: 1920x1080px (16:9 ratio) - Restaurant interior/atmosphere
- **Dish Photos**: 600x400px (3:2 ratio) - Food photography
- **Atmosphere**: 800x600px (4:3 ratio) - Interior/exterior shots
- **Logo**: 200x80px (2.5:1 ratio) - Restaurant branding

### Photo Tips:
- Use natural lighting when possible
- Style food attractively
- Show real customers enjoying meals
- Update seasonal photos regularly

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### ❌ Don't:
- Use stock photos that don't match your restaurant
- Forget to update seasonal menus
- Ignore mobile users (many reservations made on phones)
- Neglect to respond to reviews

### ✅ Do:
- Use high-quality, authentic photos
- Keep menu and pricing current
- Make reservations easy to book
- Engage with customer feedback
