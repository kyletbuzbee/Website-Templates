# Roofers & Exterior Template - Quick Start Guide

## Welcome to Your Roofing & Exterior Website!

This guide will help you customize your roofing contractor website in just a few steps. Perfect for roofing companies, siding contractors, and exterior specialists!

---

## Step 1: Emergency Response Setup

### Storm Damage Banner
**Why it's important:** Roofing emergencies happen 24/7 - this banner captures urgent leads
- **Phone number:** Use a dedicated emergency line (different from main number)
- **Call-to-action:** "We Respond 24/7!" or "Emergency Storm Repair"
- **Services:** List storm-related services prominently

### Emergency Contact Strategy:
- **Separate phone number:** Consider a Google Voice number that rings your emergency phone
- **Voicemail message:** "For storm damage emergencies, press 1. For estimates, press 2."
- **Staff training:** Ensure someone always answers emergency calls

## Step 6: Seasonal Promotions - Drive Off-Season Business

### Seasonal Offer Structure:
Each promotion needs:
- **Seasonal hook:** "Spring Roof Special", "Summer Siding Deal"
- **Specific savings:** "$500 off roof inspections", "20% off siding"
- **Time limit:** "Through August 31st"
- **Call-to-action:** "Schedule now to save"

### Seasonal Calendar:
- **Spring:** Roof inspections, storm prep, cleaning
- **Summer:** Siding deals, painting specials, deck construction
- **Fall:** Gutter services, roof repairs before winter
- **Winter:** Emergency services, indoor referrals

### Promotion Tips:
- **Limited time:** Creates urgency
- **Package deals:** Combine services for bigger discounts
- **Referral bonuses:** Reward customer referrals
- **Email list:** Build subscriber list for future offers

---

## Step 7: Pricing Transparency - Build Trust

### Pricing Table Strategy:
**Columns to Include:**
1. **Service Name** - Clear, descriptive titles
2. **Starting Price** - Your minimum price point
3. **Average Cost** - Realistic range ($X,XXX - $Y,YYY)
4. **Time Frame** - How long the project takes
5. **Get Quote Button** - Direct to contact form

### Pricing Philosophy:
- **Transparent:** No "surprise" fees
- **Honest ranges:** Don't lowball to get leads
- **Value-focused:** Explain what customers get
- **Local factors:** Note that prices vary by complexity

### Important Disclaimers:
- "Prices may vary based on material selection and roof complexity"
- "Free estimates available - no obligation"
- "Licensed contractors - fully insured"

## Media Guidelines - Professional Photography

### Image Specifications:
- **Hero Images:** 1920x1080px, professional roofing work
- **Before/After Pairs:** 600x400px each, same perspective
- **Service Photos:** 800x600px, clear action shots
- **Team Photos:** 400x400px, professional headshots

### Photography Tips:
- **Safety first:** Never climb ladders for photos
- **Weather appropriate:** Show work in context
- **Progress documentation:** Before, during, after shots
- **Equipment visible:** Show professional tools and vehicles

- Explain industry terms
