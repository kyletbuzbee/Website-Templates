# Real Estate Industry Template - Quick Start Guide

## Welcome to Your Real Estate Website!

This guide will help you customize your real estate industry template in just a few steps. Perfect for real estate agencies, brokers, property managers, and real estate professionals!

---

## Step 1: Agency Information

### What to Change:
- **Agency Name**: Replace "Summit Realty Group"
- **Tagline**: Your unique value proposition
- **License Number**: Your real estate license information
- **Service Area**: Geographic areas you serve

### Where to Find It:
- Page title and header
- Hero section
- Footer contact information
- About section

---

## Step 2: Hero Section Setup

### Hero Image
**What to do:**
1. Choose a stunning photo of luxury homes or city skyline
2. Use a high-quality image (1920x1080px recommended)
3. Show the type of properties you specialize in

**Pro tip:** Use an image that represents your target market and property types.

### Hero Text
**What to change:**
- **Main headline**: Focus on your expertise (under 10 words)
- **Subtitle**: Describe your services and market knowledge (2-3 sentences)
- **Buttons**: Update to match your services ("Search Properties", "Contact Agent")

**Example Headlines:**
- "Your Trusted Real Estate Partner"
- "Luxury Homes & Investment Properties"
- "Expert Real Estate Services Since 2008"

---

## Step 3: Property Listings

### Featured Properties
**For each property:**
1. **Property Title**: Descriptive and appealing
2. **Address**: Full location details
3. **Price**: Current listing price
4. **Details**: Bedrooms, bathrooms, square footage
5. **Features**: Key selling points and amenities

### Property Tips:
- Feature 4-6 current listings
- Include variety (houses, condos, commercial)
- Update pricing and status regularly
- Add professional property photos

---

## Step 4: Agent Profiles

### Team Information
**For each agent:**
- **Professional headshot**: High-quality photo
- **Bio**: Experience, specialties, achievements
- **Contact information**: Phone, email, social media
- **Statistics**: Sales closed, experience, specialties

### Agent Tips:
- Include 3-6 top-producing agents
- Highlight different specialties (residential, commercial, luxury)
- Add professional certifications and awards
- Include social media links for credibility

---

## Step 5: Services Section

### Service Offerings
**What to include:**
- Residential sales and purchases
- Commercial real estate services
- Property management
- Market analysis and consulting
- Investment property guidance

### Service Customization:
- Add your specific service packages
- Include pricing or "starting at" ranges
- Highlight unique offerings
- Add testimonials for each service

---

## Step 6: Property Search

### Search Functionality
**Customize filters:**
- Property types you specialize in
- Price ranges for your market
- Bedroom/bathroom options
- Neighborhoods you serve

### Search Tips:
- Set realistic price ranges for your area
- Include all major property types
- Add popular neighborhoods first
- Consider adding advanced filters (pool, garage, etc.)

---

## Step 7: Testimonials & Reviews

### Client Reviews
**What to include:**
- Recent successful transactions
- Different property types represented
- Mix of buyer and seller experiences
- Both residential and commercial clients

### Getting Reviews:
- Request reviews after closing
- Use Zillow, Realtor.com review systems
- Ask for Google My Business reviews
- Follow up with satisfied clients

---

## Step 8: Contact Information

### Essential Details:
- Main office phone number
- Email address for inquiries
- Physical office address
- Individual agent contact information

### Additional Info:
- Office hours and availability
- Service areas and territories
- Languages spoken
- Emergency contact numbers

---

## Media Guidelines

### Image Sizes & Types:
- **Hero Image**: 1920x1080px (16:9 ratio) - Luxury homes/city skyline
- **Property Photos**: 800x600px (4:3 ratio) - Professional property shots
- **Agent Photos**: 400x400px (1:1 ratio) - Professional headshots
- **Logo**: 200x80px (2.5:1 ratio) - Agency branding

### Photo Tips:
- Use professional real estate photography
- Show properties in their best light
- Include lifestyle shots when appropriate
- Update agent photos regularly

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### ❌ Don't:
- Use outdated or off-market listings
- Forget to update agent information
- Include unlicensed team members
- Use stock photos that don't represent your area

### ✅ Do:
- Verify all license information is current
- Update property statuses immediately
- Respond to inquiries within 24 hours
- Maintain professional online presence
