# Starter Template - Quick Start Guide

## Welcome to Your Business Website!

This guide will help you customize your Starter template website in just a few simple steps. Perfect for small businesses, consultants, and service providers!

---

## Step 1: Basic Company Information

### What to Change:
- **Company Name**: Replace "Acme Solutions" everywhere
- **Tagline**: Create a short, memorable phrase
- **Phone & Email**: Your business contact details
- **Address**: Your business location (optional)

### Where to Find It:
- Page title and header
- Footer contact information
- About section
- Contact form confirmation

---

## Step 2: Hero Section Setup

### Hero Image
**What to do:**
1. Choose a professional photo showing your team or work
2. Use a high-quality image (1920x1080px recommended)
3. Make sure it represents your brand well

**Pro tip:** Use an image that shows the results you deliver to clients.

### Hero Text
**What to change:**
- **Main headline**: Focus on your biggest benefit (under 10 words)
- **Subtitle**: Explain what makes you different (2-3 sentences)
- **Buttons**: Update to match your services

**Example Headlines:**
- "Grow Your Business with Expert Guidance"
- "Professional Services That Drive Results"
- "Your Success is Our Top Priority"

---

## Step 3: Services Section

### For Each Service:
1. **Service Title**: Clear, benefit-focused (2-5 words)
2. **Description**: 1-2 sentences explaining what you do
3. **Icon**: Choose relevant icon for each service
4. **Link**: Connect to service detail pages

### Service Ideas:
- Business Consulting
- Digital Marketing
- Financial Planning
- Technology Solutions
- HR Support
- Legal Services
- Training & Development
- Customer Service

**Pro tip:** Focus on 4-6 core services. Too many can confuse visitors.

---

## Step 4: About Section

### Content to Update:
- **Company story**: When/why you started, your mission
- **Team photo**: Professional group photo
- **Statistics**: Years in business, clients served, success metrics
- **Call-to-action**: Link to contact or services

### Statistics Ideas:
- Years in business
- Clients served
- Projects completed
- Client satisfaction rate
- Awards or certifications

---

## Step 5: Testimonials

### What Makes a Good Testimonial:
- Specific results mentioned
- Client name and business included
- Recent (within 2 years)
- Different types of projects

### How to Get Testimonials:
- Ask satisfied clients via email
- Include in project completion emails
- Use LinkedIn or Google Review requests
- Offer incentives for reviews

### Display Tips:
- Use real client names and businesses
- Include photos if available
- Keep quotes concise (2-3 sentences)
- Show variety in client types

---

## Step 6: Blog Section

### Blog Post Ideas:
- Industry tips and trends
- How-to guides for your clients
- Case studies and success stories
- Company news and updates
- Educational content

### For Each Post:
1. **Title**: Click-worthy and descriptive
2. **Excerpt**: First 2-3 sentences of your post
3. **Image**: Relevant, high-quality photo
4. **Category**: Marketing, Finance, Management, etc.
5. **Read time**: Estimate based on word count

**Pro tip:** Start with 3-5 posts. Add more regularly to show expertise.

---

## Step 7: Contact Form

### Form Fields (Keep These):
- First Name & Last Name (required)
- Email Address (required)
- Phone Number (optional)
- Service Interest (dropdown)
- Message (text area)
- Newsletter signup (checkbox)

### Service Options:
Match your services section exactly. Add "General Inquiry" as the last option.

---

## Step 8: Footer Customization

### Contact Information:
- Phone number (clickable)
- Email address (mailto link)
- Business address
- Business hours

### Links to Include:
- Service pages
- About page
- Blog
- Privacy Policy
- Terms of Service

### Social Media:
- Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter
- Only include accounts you actively maintain

---

## Media Guidelines

### Image Sizes & Formats:
- **Hero Image**: 1920x1080px (16:9 ratio)
- **About Image**: 800x600px (4:3 ratio)
- **Blog Images**: 800x450px (16:9 ratio)
- **Logo**: 200x80px (2.5:1 ratio)
- **Service Icons**: 64x64px (square)

### File Formats:
- Photos: JPG or WebP (highest quality)
- Icons: SVG preferred, PNG acceptable
- Logo: PNG or SVG (transparent background)
- Max file size: 2MB for hero, 500KB for others

### Keywords:
- Your industry + "services"
- Your location + "business"
- Key service terms
- Problem you solve + "solutions"

---

## Content Writing Tips

### Headlines:
- Use active words (Grow, Create, Transform)
- Focus on benefits, not features
- Keep under 10 words when possible

### Body Text:
- Write in second person ("you" not "we")
- Use short sentences and paragraphs
- Include specific numbers and results
- End with clear calls-to-action

### Calls-to-Action:
- "Get Started Today"
- "Contact Us Now"
- "Learn More"
- "Schedule Consultation"
- "Download Guide"

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### ❌ Don't:
- Use generic placeholder text
- Include blurry or low-quality images
- Forget to update contact information
- Make paragraphs too long
- Use industry jargon without explanation

### ✅ Do:
- Use specific, benefit-focused language
- Include social proof (testimonials, stats)
- Make it easy to contact you
- Use consistent branding
- Test all links and forms
