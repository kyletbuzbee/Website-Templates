# Minimal Template - Quick Start Guide

## Welcome to Your Simple Business Website!

This guide will help you customize your Minimal template website in just a few minutes. Perfect for businesses that want to get online quickly!

---

## Step 1: Basic Information

### What to Change:
- **Company Name**: Replace "Simple Solutions" everywhere
- **Tagline**: Create a short, memorable phrase
- **Phone & Email**: Your business contact details

### Where to Find It:
- Page title and header
- Footer contact information
- About section

---

## Step 2: Hero Section

### Hero Image
**What to do:**
1. Choose a professional photo representing your business
2. Use a high-quality image (1920x1080px recommended)
3. Keep it simple and clean

### Hero Text
**What to change:**
- **Main headline**: Your main benefit (under 8 words)
- **Subtitle**: What you do (1-2 sentences)
- **Buttons**: Update to match your services

**Example Headlines:**
- "Simple Solutions for Your Business"
- "Professional Services Made Easy"
- "Get Your Business Online Today"

---

## Step 3: Services Section

### For Each Service:
1. **Service Title**: Clear and simple (2-4 words)
2. **Description**: 1 sentence explaining what you do
3. **Icon**: Choose a relevant icon

### Keep It Simple:
Focus on 3-4 core services maximum. Less is more!

---

## Step 4: About Section

### Content to Update:
- **Company description**: Who you are and what you do
- **Team photo**: Professional photo
- **Statistics**: 3-4 key numbers
- **Call-to-action**: Link to contact

---

## Step 5: Contact Form

### Form Fields:
- First Name & Last Name (required)
- Email Address (required)
- Phone Number (optional)
- Message (text area)

### Service Options:
Match your services section exactly.

---

## Media Guidelines

### Image Sizes:
- **Hero Image**: 1920x1080px (16:9 ratio)
- **About Image**: 800x600px (4:3 ratio)
- **Logo**: 200x80px (2.5:1 ratio)
- **Service Icons**: 64x64px (square)

---

## Quick Launch Checklist

- [ ] Company name updated
- [ ] Hero image and text customized
- [ ] Services section completed
- [ ] About section with photo
- [ ] Contact form configured
- [ ] All images added

---

## Need Help?

### Content Tips:
- Keep text short and clear
- Use simple language
- Focus on benefits
- Include your contact info

### Technical Tips:
- Test on phone and desktop
- Check that forms work
- Ensure images load quickly
